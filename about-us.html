Our Story

At Wulf Arms, we started as an American small family-owned business in 2023 with the focus of offering a friendly and professional, customer oriented experience that curates some of the finest weapons, gear and accessories with a specific focus of being a premier tactical outfitter. Our commitment to you is offering quality American and European firearms and accessories at affordable prices whether for duty to or personal defense of the homeland.

Our Productss

We offer a wide range of products, from firearms, parts and accessories, ammunition, gear and clothing, and electronics. Our selection is carefully curated to ensure that we only offer the best for professionals, sportsmen, and the citizen soldier.
